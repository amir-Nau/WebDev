How We Started

Shoes are the finishing touch on any any outfit and it is important to complete a look with the perfect pair.

Carrie and I met two years ago at Gucci Runaway Show in Milan. We were sitting together in the same row. I wasn't able to buy any expensive outfit for the night, because I spent all my money on the ticket. However, I borrowed a bit from my college savings and got myself a new pair of Manolo Blahnik. I decorated them with old-style vintage beads. Carrie noticed my shoes as we started taking our seats. She couldn't help but talking about how she like my shoes through the whole show. We met a couple of times after that and decided to open our own designer shoe company.
